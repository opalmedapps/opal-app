What is historical delay?
0-15 min
 
 80%
Percentage is calculated on the total number of appointments of similar type, date and time from the last 3 months (80% would indicate that 80% of all similar appointments were delayed by 0 to 15 minutes).

Delay is the number of minutes by which patient's scheduled appointment was late/postponed. It is calculated by subtracting treatment time from scheduled time (if the scheduled time is at 2:00 and the patient is seen at 2:10, then the delay is 10 minutes).
